About

Established in 2006, Fuel Motion Inc. (FMI) is a California based company that specializes in the development of software and technology for energy storage and energy management systems. FMI has created a complete Energy Management System (EMS) that is powered by Lithium-ion batteries.

Our EMS is directly integrated with the power supply to provide seamless energy monitoring and backup power. With 24/7 cloud based monitoring, this is a complete solution for disruption free energy to any residential or commercial application. In the past, we have developed software for Hybrid conversion kits, Electric drive train conversion, and for Battery Management Systems.

Overview

Zoreza Energy Managed Storage (EMS) is a power system driven by Utility supplied Electricity to deliver continuous on-line, defect free electric power. It is a state of the art Battery Management System (BMS) technology, with a very long lasting (10 years) high tech Lithium batteries (the one used in Electric vehicle)

It gives 10 KW of pure Sine wave output power that can run five air conditioners with other load and save energy. During power outage it works as a backup for load shedding, several hours.A paradigm shift in using Lithium (LiFePO4) instead of conventional Lead Acid batteries for storage, is an innovation of Zoreza EMS that comes from USA

Zoreza EMS is equipped with an android tablet, Wi-Fi, cloud connectivity and 24/7 monitoring from anywhere in the World
